Andrew Park



Hello there! I'm Andrew Park, a software developer and instructor based in Corona, CA.

My expertise is in full-stack app development, with a focus on front-end design and user experience.
I'm experienced in a variety of languages and frameworks, including C#, Java, Python, C++, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Typescript, and Dart/Flutter.
I also have extensive experience in the Unity game engine, as well as integration with a variety of services like Firebase.

With a background in teaching along with the creative arts, I'm driven by a lifelong desire to combine my interests with my technical knowledge to create experiences that will improve the lives of others.
