Tetro

Tetro is my own personal take on the classic tetromino-based puzzler which took roughly two weeks from conception to bring to a playable state. I was not satisfied by the existing offerings on the app store and so went about building this project with the goal of making a variant that I would enjoy playing and looking at. I designed the UI initially in Figma before eventually implementing everything in Flutter with a custom neuomorphic widget library I wrote to accomodate the requirements of the project.

Currently this app lives exclusively on my personal phone, but I may eventually look to release the app on the app stores for iOS and Android once I have refined and ironed out some necessary adjustments. Here is a video showing some gameplay of how it runs:
