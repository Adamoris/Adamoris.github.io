Aristo

Aristo is the name of the primary game project that I am currently actively working on. The image that you see above is specifically from an earlier build whose concept was taken from conception to playable with a period of a week! I was responsible for all parts of the project's initial creation apart from the quality assurance testing. This particular variant of Aristo has since been renamed to Aristo Legacy and a more complete variant of the game is in development. You can get this initial prototype here: https://volitare.itch.io/aristo

I will be posting more information along with a playable build of the expanded version here!
